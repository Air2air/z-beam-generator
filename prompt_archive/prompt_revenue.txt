**Research and Benchmarks (Before Calculations - *Crucial*):**

For *each* of the following cost and revenue elements, you *must* conduct research and provide:

1. **Range:**  A realistic range of values.
2. **Source:**  Specific sources for your data (e.g., URLs, company names, industry reports).  Vague references are not acceptable.
3. **Justification:**  Explain *why* the values in your range are realistic for Z-Beam's context (California, laser cleaning services, target clients, etc.).  Consider factors like experience level of technicians, type of laser equipment, and local market conditions.

**Cost Elements (Monthly):**

* **Van (Lease, Insurance, Fuel):**

* **Other Overhead (Per Technician):** Include costs like mobile phone, uniform, software subscriptions, small tools, and any other recurring expenses directly related to the technician's operation.

**Revenue Elements:**

* **Utilization Rate:** Research industry benchmarks for utilization rates in similar mobile service businesses.  Consider travel time, equipment maintenance, administrative tasks, and potential downtime between jobs.
