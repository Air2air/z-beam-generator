
** Technical **
My new website z-beam.com is built in Nextjs as a static site, using Cloudinary for images.   

** Serps **
z-beam.com was launched with a new domain in January 2025.  It is intended to be highly searchable, with 240 pages of which only 87 are currently indexed.  The site does not comprehensively adhere to Google guidelines yet.

** Content **
The site's intent is to be a public service repository about laser cleaning, a new industry.   It should have the widest possible variety of content sections related to laser cleaning.  Currently it has:

1. Pages devoted to a specific material, e.g Titanium, and every related aspect of laser cleaning of this material.
2. Pages devoted to specific laser cleaning applications, e.g Non-destructive testing of Stainless Steel tanks.  

Each page has several sections including tables, charts, images and text.  Subjects are relevant only to the page topic and include applications, materials science, laser cleaning settings, substrate performance etcetera.  Each page will combine the sections together.

The content should always be supremely valuable to professionals looking for guidance on how to clean the affected substrate materials in their application, and should become the industry reference guide.  

For example, a professional trying to laser clean titanium should come to the z-beam.com page from Google to get all the information they would possibly need to successfully analyze and clean the substrate.
