## What is Laser Cleaning
Laser cleaning uses high-intensity laser beams to remove rust, paint, grease, and contaminants without damaging substrates, offering superior **environmental**, **efficiency**, **speed**, and **cost** benefits over legacy methods like sandblasting and chemical cleaning. It produces minimal waste, captured by fume extractors, and eliminates toxic solvents, reducing compliance costs. Non-contact precision enhances efficiency, cutting cleaning time by 25% and costs by 30–50% compared to labor-intensive, risky legacy methods. Z-Beam’s Netalux Jango 300 delivers these advantages, ensuring high-quality results.
