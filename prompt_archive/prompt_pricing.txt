## Simplified Pricing Table Prompt

Craft a clear pricing table for Z-Beam, a mobile laser cleaning service in Northern California, based on substrates and contamination levels within the laser cleaning industry. Output in HTML format.

### Content Guidelines  
- **Objective**: Create a pricing table for Z-Beam’s website targeting industrial clients, facility managers, and decision-makers in Northern California, showing cost ranges by substrate and contamination level.  
- **Structure**:  
  - **Table**: Include columns: Substrate, Per sq ft (Range for Light to Heavy Contamination), Difficulty (Rating for Light to Heavy Contamination).  
  - **Rows**: Group these substrates by cleaning effort:  
    - Light Metals: Aluminum, Magnesium  
    - Standard Metals: Brass, Copper  
    - Ferrous Metals: Cast Iron, Carbon Steel, Galvanized Steel, Stainless Steel, Tool Steel  
    - Heavy Alloys: Cobalt Chromium, Molybdenum, Lead, Nickel Alloy, Titanium  
    - Masonry: Stone, Concrete, Masonry  
    - Wood & Organics: Wood, Other Organics (e.g., bamboo, rubber)  
    with each row showing per sq ft ranges and difficulty for Light (e.g., dust) to Heavy (e.g., thick coatings) contamination.  
  - **Note**: Add a short paragraph below explaining pricing (e.g., contamination and size impact), a custom quote call-to-action, and the difficulty scale (1–5).  
- **Research Basis**:  
  - Use laser cleaning industry sources (e.g., Photonics Media, Industrial Laser Solutions, Laser Focus World, Mordor Intelligence, SPIE) for substrates, trends, and national per sq ft rates.  
  - Exclude non-laser cleaning data (e.g., Homewyse).  
  - Apply a 30% premium for Northern California costs, reflecting labor (e.g., $30–$50/hour, BLS), fuel (e.g., $4.50/gallon), and demand (e.g., tech, preservation).  

### Content Specifications  
- **Substrates**: Group materials by contaminants and effort (e.g., ferrous metals: rust, moderate; light metals: oxides, easy).  
- **Contamination**: Light (e.g., dust, minimal effort) to Heavy (e.g., thick coatings, high effort).  
- **Pricing**:

- **Difficulty**: Rate 1–5 (1 = Easy, 5 = Hard) per group, spanning Light to Heavy (e.g., 2–4).  
- Keep it tied to substrates, contamination, and Northern California costs.
