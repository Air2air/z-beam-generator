Brass Laser Cleaning Dataset
================================================================================

Description: Brass, as non-ferrous metal, shows high reflectivity during laser cleaning, so treatment removes surface oxidation and contamination from marine or plumbing uses without deep substrate damage. Because of its good corrosion resistance and thermal shock tolerance, process restores uniformity on surfaces effectively, and this sets brass apart from ferrous alternatives that suffer more from heat buildup.
