Description: The key to Indium is its remarkably low melting point, which sets it apart from tougher metals by enabling soft, reliable seals in electronics and aerospace components. We've found it perfect for delicate soldering tasks where high heat could ruin sensitive parts, but always keep processing temperatures gentle to maintain its integrity.
