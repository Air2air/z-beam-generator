# TiN/Ceramic Coatings

## APPEARANCE
- TiN: GOLD/BRONZE metallic sheen (must be visible)
- Contamination = thin discoloration, NOT chunks or 3D deposits

## DISTRIBUTION
- Natural variation: heavier in crevices, lighter on high points
- NOT uniform coating

## AFTER CLEANING
- Gold TiN color fully restored
- Shows wear marks (scratches) - NOT factory-new
