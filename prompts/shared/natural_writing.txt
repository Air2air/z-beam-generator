=== NATURAL WRITING PRINCIPLES ===
- Write like explaining to a knowledgeable colleague
- Use concrete examples and specific use cases
- Ground content in real-world applications
- Compare to familiar alternatives when useful
- Describe behavior in context ("Under X condition, material does Y")
- Vary vocabulary naturally (don't repeat key terms)
- Mix sentence lengths naturally
- Alternate structures (simple, compound, complex)
- Avoid formulaic patterns
